Learn from Jeff Hodge, comedian/author/producer/writer and teacher.  Whether your goal is to be a stand up comedian, a comedy writer, bring humor to public speaking, or simply inject more humor into your daily life, Jeff’s class is definitely the class to attend.

He provides a space to create, experiment and find your own comedy voice.

Jeff Hodge, the founder of the Jeff Hodge School Of Comedy, has been a standup comedian for 25+ years.  He has spent much of that time touring on the road and producing live comedy events, all over the USA.  Jeff has also written for some of the biggest names in comedy from George Lopez, Arsenio Hall to Steve Harvey.

Now Jeff uses his experience and joke writing skills to teach other aspiring comedians and humor writers how to write and perform a joke in a very competitive but rewarding business.
Jeff breaks down the comedy writing and performing process.  He teaches you all the techniques & knowledge used by working comedians on all different levels to create great comedy material.

You'll go from having an idea to writing and performing a 5-minute set at the Ice House Comedy Club in just 5 weeks!  Jeff’s classes are fun, informative and effective.
Benefits of taking Jeff Hodge School Of Comedy classes:

*  Best value and easy payment plan!

* Learning from a working standup comedian, so his techniques and info is current

* As you advance in your career, you may get an opportunity to perform in the various comedy shows that Jeff produces throughout California.
Another benefit of taking Jeff Hodge School Of Comedy classes … You will perform at the legendary Ice House Comedy Club in Pasadena for your showcase.  (The Ice House Comedy Club is the oldest comedy club in America.

Any comedian who is somebody in comedy today or yesterday has graced the main stage of the Ice House Comedy Club at some time in their comedy career).
