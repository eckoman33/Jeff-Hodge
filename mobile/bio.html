 JEFF HODGE

Comedian/Actor/Producer/Author/Host

yeahmonentertainment@gmail.com

Jeff Hodge, a native of the U.S. Virgin Islands, is an entertainment industry veteran. He has performed stand up comedy across the world, from America’s hottest clubs to a USO Comedy Tour of the Middle East, where he entertained thousands of troops from all branches of the United States Military and other Coalition Forces. He has opened for David Sanborn, George Lopez and Arsenio Hall.

As an actor, Jeff has appeared in television and films such as The Tonight Show, The Jamie Foxx Show, Are You Smarter Than A 5th Grader, Deuce Bigalow: Male Gigolo and Crocodile Dundee in L.A.

As a writer, Jeff has authored several, successful relationship books including 101+ Ways To Keep A Man, 101+To Tell When the Relationship Is Over and 101+ Ways To Tell If The Person Your Dating Is Crazy. His most recent book, ‘Road Trippin’: The Life and Times of A Comic On The Run looks over Jeff’s early years on the road as a standup comic.

Jeff is also a producer of live comedy and films. His short films Kindergarten and Comedy Traffic School won awards at independent film festivals. Currently, he produces Jeff Hodge Car Diaries, a web-series that follows Jeff as he drives an Uber through the jungle of Los Angeles. His ‘Ambassadors of Comedy’, which features America’s funniest, foreign comedians, tours throughout the USA, and his monthly residency (Jeff Hodge & Friends Comedy Jam) at the world-renowned Ice House Comedy Club in Pasadena, California brings Jeff and his exclusive array of the hottest comics to the spotlight.
